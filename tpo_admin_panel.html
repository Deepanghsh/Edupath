Readiness Score Algorithm
The Readiness Score (0–100) is a heuristic computed as:
Score = (CGPA/10 × 35) + (DSA_Marks/100 × 30) + (OOP_Marks/100 × 25) + (Backlog_Penalty × 10)
Backlog_Penalty = max(0, 1 − active_backlogs × 0.25). Students with a strong skill match for in-demand roles receive a +5 bonus.
